Refund Policy For ApexArray

Damaged Products:

  • If you receive a damaged or defective product, provide clear photographic evidence within 24 hours.
  • Contact customer support at  apexarraystore@gmail.com  with order number and evidence.
  • We'll gladly provide a refund or replacement.

Refund Eligibility Timeframe:

  • Clients eligible for refund within 30 Days of purchase.
  • Refund requests after this timeframe won't be considered.
  • Review your order carefully and contact us immediately for a refund within specified timeframe.

How to Request a Refund:

Contact customer support at apexarraystore@gmail.com with:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photographic evidence (for damaged products)
  • Reason for refund request
  • Contact information

Refund Process:

  • Once approved, we'll initiate the refund process.
  • Refunds issued to original payment method.
  • Allow 5 business days for refund to reflect in your account.

Exceptions:

No refunds for products damaged due to mishandling, misuse, or failure to follow care instructions.

Contact Us:

If you have questions or concerns regarding our refund policy, contact customer support at apexarraystore@gmail.com.
